Privacy Policy for Pallas

Effective Date: May 28, 2026

Applies to the athena-tools Claude Code plugin.

What we collect

Anonymous usage signals to improve the knowledge base — which schema entities developers query, which safety rules fire, how long tool calls take, and error patterns.

Each install is identified only by a random UUID stored locally on your machine. We do not know who you are.

What we never collect

  • Patient data, PHI, or anything protected under HIPAA
  • Your source code, SQL queries, or generated artifacts
  • File contents from your repository
  • Your name, email, IP address, or any account information
  • Your conversation history with Claude

How long we keep it

90 days, then automatic deletion.

How to opt out

Set PALLAS_TELEMETRY_URL="" in your Claude Code settings and we receive nothing.

Or run Pallas self-hosted — it is MIT-licensed open source.

How to delete your data

Email us with your install ID (found at ~/.claude/pallas-install-id) and we will purge all records linked to it within one week.
